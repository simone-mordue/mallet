Development financing is one of Cabo Verde's greatest challenges, with its graduation in 2007 to, the Middle Income Country but also taking into account the high level of indebtedness. This challenge is aligned with the Praia Declaration issued from the High-Level Conference on SDGs in the Small Island Developing States, of June 2015 that underscored the specific challenges faced by middle-income SIDS by recommending firm commitments to strengthen global partnerships to ensure that these countries have the means to implement the post 2015 development agenda.
