Promoting sustainable agriculture is one of the biggest challenges of Cabo Verde. Contributing to the appreciation of investments already carried out, particularly on mobilization of water, land use, research with integrated water re- sources management, including the use of treated wastewater and desalinated water produced with moderate energy costs for renewable energies, the mobilization of the youth population, and the paradigm shift for entrepreneurship. In addition, the appreciation of modern technologies and in particular the quality assurance, production regularity, in connection with the tourist market, in an efficient transportation context.
