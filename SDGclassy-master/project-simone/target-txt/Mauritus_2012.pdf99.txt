2.22 Health
An average of 574 new cases of HIV/AIDS has been reported annually for the 2004-2011 period, with
74.2% of all cases of HIV/AIDS registered since 1987 being Injecting Drug Users (Ministry of Health and
Quality of Life, 2011). A cumulative total of 5,157 HIV cases have been detected as at November 2011
(Health Statistics, 2011). Apart from HIV/AIDS, another major health  challenge  is posed by the
increasing level of non-communicable diseases such as obesity, diabetes, cardiac problems and cancer
