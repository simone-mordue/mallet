The Ministry of Sugar in conjunction with the Ministry of Primary Industries are also focused on
enhancing diversification projects to ensure a broader income base for cane farmers through import
substitution and export promotions programme. There is a growing lack of confidence amongst farmers
within the cane belt to plant cane. This has seen a mass exodus of people from the cane farming belts to the
towns and cities in search of a more secure income, education, and livelihood. This presents a major
 challenge  for the industry, with an ageing population left to tend the farms. The solution to this is to make
sugarcane farming attractive again
