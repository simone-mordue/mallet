  5.5    Environment sustainability
Despite this relatively strong policy framework developed under the NEMs and subsequent
programs, a major  challenge  would be to reverse the loss of environmental resources. The
proportion of land covered by forests has increased form 35% (2000) to 60% (2005) a
reflection of an aggressive approach to promote reafforestation. The share of the population
with access to safe water continues to increase although 100% coverage has not yet been
achieved
