Challenges
To have more equitable, socially just and inclusive outcomes, emphasis is needed to
ensure that women have equal rights to access, ownership, control of land and
economic opportunities, these we strong interlinkages with SDGs 2, 3 and 8. Steps
need to be taken to  challenge  and remove the barriers women face in getting land
tenure and access to productive resources. Economic opportunities include having
the requisite representation of females on public and private sector boards, as well
as improved representation in the Parliament (including the upper and lower
houses)
