            Implementation of Agenda 2030
Following the accession of Guinea on the agenda ODD 2030, authorities at the highest level countries have initiated a process of
updating the national planning framework for aligning the vision 2040 and the PNDES2016-2020 on the new agenda
development of 2030. the Government is particularly aware that the implementation of ODD is a huge  challenge  and to succeed,
it is necessary not only to raise the profile ODD and New Dealpar all stakeholders at central and decentralized levels, civil society
and the private sector, but also to share the optimum frameworks and strategies to achieve them
